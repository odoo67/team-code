Overview

You can Create an Employee, based on the configuration approvals will be sent to the respective HR manager.
Purpose - There will be no permissions in Odoo for employee creation, however approvals are provided here to minimise perplexity.

Features

Under Employee Settings, there is a checkbox; if checked, approvals will be processed.
In the user master, choose the HR Manager checkbox to determine who should get approvals.
After they are created, employees will not be visible at all. In the meantime, you need to click the Send for approval option.
After seeing the mail, the mapped HR manager can click the "Approve" button to take them to the employee page.
Being approved the employee will be seen everywhere.
